Trading Coaching

What makes trading consistently profitable? A solid knowledge base and the right psychological approach. Therefore, either you lack sufficient knowledge, or your behavior in the market is ineffective. That’s exactly what we will improve.

How does it work?

During the first free session, we will get acquainted and identify the core problem. Then, we will work on resolving it. This may involve regular structured lessons or focusing on a specific topic to address it thoroughly. What is likely to happen next:

We will clearly define what needs to be worked on

You will gain a new perspective on the markets

Your trading results should improve

What format of training do I offer?

We analyze

I personally review your trading journal, uncover recurring mistakes, and guide you step by step toward smarter decisions and consistent, long-term trading improvement.

Improvement

We work on the areas you'd like to improve, as well as the issues I identify after reviewing your trading journal.

Backtesting

Once we’ve addressed the key issues and I’ve provided you with new tools or approaches, you begin backtesting to reinforce and validate the changes.

Final check

After you've finished backtesting I'm going to check your backtest and see if you're ready to go.

Frequently asked questions

Is the first session free?

Yes. The initial call is completely free and has no time limit. We get to know each other, identify your challenges, and discuss how we can address them most effectively.

When will I see results?

There is no universal answer—it depends on your current level. If you’re a beginner, there’s more groundwork to cover. If you’re already advanced, one or two sessions might be enough.

Do you trade yourself?

Yes, I actively trade as well. You can find more details about me on the "About" page, and you’re welcome to ask any questions during the first free call.

How can I schedule a call?

Either directly on this page or by contacting me through any of my social media accounts.

Will the session be with you or someone else?

Only with me.

If I start from zero what should I do ?

You can either study the free material available online or study with me. A full course costs $1000. There’s no time limit for the course — I will guide you for as long as necessary.
